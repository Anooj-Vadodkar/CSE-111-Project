About This Webpage

Welcome to our final project for the CSE 111: Database Systems course!.

Browse through the vast history of games and how successful they were through this website. Data goes up to 2016, with more on the way as we get it.

If there's a game you're thinking belongs on here but you can't find it through the search function, feel free to send it in to us over here. We will review the submission and, if appropriate, add it to our database. You can also send in any bugs or errors you find over there.
